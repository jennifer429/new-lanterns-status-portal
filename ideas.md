# Design Ideas for New Lanterns PACS Status Portal

> Design exploration notes from early brainstorming sessions. These are reference concepts, not active plans.

## Design Direction: Clinical Modernism

**Chosen approach** — clean hierarchy with medical-grade precision, data-first information architecture.

- **Primary palette:** Deep teal (#0D7377) for trust, warm amber (#F4A261) for progress/success states
- **Layout:** Left-aligned timeline navigation with 30/70 split content panels
- **Typography:** Inter for body (400/500), Manrope for headings (600/700)
- **Motion:** Subtle fade-up (300ms), organic easing on progress bars

## Alternative Concepts (Not Pursued)

- **Soft Brutalism:** Bold geometric shapes, navy + coral, stacked cards. More aggressive but less clinical.
- **Airy Minimalism:** Lavender-to-blue gradients, glassmorphic cards, centered 900px column. Too ethereal for healthcare ops.

## Future Enhancements to Consider

- Real-time WebSocket updates for activity feed
- File preview in portal (instead of redirect to Drive)
- Bulk organization creation for PMs
- Analytics dashboard (completion rates, time-to-complete, bottlenecks)

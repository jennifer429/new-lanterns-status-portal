**Design Movement:** Clinical Modernism with Data Visualization Focus

**Core Principles:**
- Clean hierarchy with medical-grade precision
- Data-first information architecture
- Trust-building through transparency and clarity
- Professional yet approachable healthcare aesthetic

**Color Philosophy:**
Deep teal (#0D7377) as primary for trust and medical professionalism, paired with warm amber (#F4A261) for progress indicators and success states. Neutral grays for backgrounds with subtle blue undertones to maintain clinical feel. Color conveys status: teal for active/in-progress, amber for milestones achieved, soft red for attention needed.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Left-aligned timeline navigation with expanding content panels. The timeline acts as the spine of the interface, always visible, with detailed task breakdowns appearing in the main content area. Asymmetric grid with 30/70 split creates visual interest while maintaining functional clarity.

**Signature Elements:**
- Vertical progress timeline with animated connection lines
- Soft-edged cards with subtle elevation shadows
- Circular progress indicators with percentage overlays

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Smooth, purposeful animations that mirror medical precision. Hover states reveal additional context without overwhelming. Click interactions expand details inline rather than navigating away. Progress updates animate in with gentle fade-up motion.

**Animation:**
Entrance animations use subtle fade-up with 300ms duration. Progress bars fill with easing curves that feel organic. Timeline connections draw in sequentially. All motion reinforces the sense of forward progress through the onboarding journey.

**Typography System:**
Inter for body text (400/500 weights) paired with Manrope (600/700) for headings. Headings use slightly expanded letter-spacing for authority. Numeric data uses tabular figures for alignment. Clear hierarchy: 32px for page title, 24px for stage headers, 16px for body, 14px for metadata.

**Design Movement:** Soft Brutalism with Healthcare Warmth

**Core Principles:**
- Bold geometric shapes with rounded corners for approachability
- High contrast for accessibility and clarity
- Honest, direct communication through design
- Functional beauty without unnecessary decoration

**Color Philosophy:**
Rich navy (#1A2B4A) as foundation, creating depth and seriousness. Soft coral (#FF6B6B) for CTAs and urgent actions. Pale sage (#E8F5E9) for completed states, conveying calm resolution. Backgrounds use off-white (#F8F9FA) to reduce eye strain during extended viewing. Color is used sparingly but intentionally to guide attention.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Stacked card system with bold section dividers. Each stage is a distinct, chunky card with generous padding. Cards stack vertically with clear separation, creating a scrollable journey. Sidebar contains quick-jump navigation with bold typography. Grid-based but feels organic through varied card heights.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thick horizontal progress bars with rounded ends
- Bold sans-serif stage numbers in circles
- Chunky buttons with high contrast
- Status badges with strong borders

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Immediate, confident feedback. Buttons depress slightly on click. Cards lift on hover. Completed items have a satisfying checkmark animation. Everything feels tactile and responsive. No ambiguity in interactive states.

**Animation:**
Bold entrance animations with slight overshoot (spring physics). Progress bars fill with momentum, not linear timing. Checkmarks draw in with a flourish. Page transitions slide with purpose. All motion feels energetic but controlled, matching the confidence of healthcare professionals.

**Typography System:**
DM Sans (500/700) for all text, creating unity and strength. Headings are bold and large (40px for hero, 28px for sections). Body text is 17px for readability. Tight line-height (1.4) for headings, generous (1.7) for body. Numbers are oversized and bold to emphasize progress metrics.

<text>
**Design Movement:** Airy Minimalism with Gradient Accents

**Core Principles:**
- Generous whitespace as a design element
- Subtle depth through layering and transparency
- Elegant simplicity that respects user focus
- Progressive disclosure of complexity

**Color Philosophy:**
Soft gradient from lavender (#9D84B7) to sky blue (#5DADE2) as the primary brand expression, used sparingly in headers and key CTAs. Most of the interface lives in shades of gray (#F5F7FA to #2C3E50) with the gradient serving as visual punctuation. Completed states glow with soft green (#52C41A), pending items remain neutral. The gradient represents the journey from start to completion.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Centered content column (max 900px) with floating navigation dots on the left edge. Each stage occupies a full viewport height, encouraging focused scrolling. Horizontal rule dividers are gradient-faded. Content breathes with 80px vertical spacing between major sections. Cards float with subtle shadows and blur effects.

**Signature Elements:**
- Gradient-filled progress circles
- Glassmorphic cards with backdrop blur
- Floating action buttons with gradient backgrounds
- Micro-interactions with elastic easing

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Whisper-quiet interactions that reward attention. Hover states brighten slightly. Clicks trigger gentle ripple effects. Scrolling reveals content with parallax subtlety. The interface feels light and effortless, never demanding but always responsive.

**Animation:**
Delicate entrance animations with staggered timing (100ms delays between elements). Progress updates morph smoothly with elastic easing. Gradient backgrounds shift subtly on scroll. All motion is understated—animations last 400-600ms with gentle curves. The effect is calming and refined.

**Typography System:**
Poppins (300/400/600) for its geometric warmth and excellent readability. Headings use 600 weight at 36px with generous letter-spacing (0.02em). Body text is 300 weight at 16px with relaxed line-height (1.8). Metadata uses 400 weight at 14px in muted gray. The light weights create an airy, modern feel.
